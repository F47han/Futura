Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about plans, pricing models, or technical database compliance?

Is there a free trial period available?

Yes. All paid subscriptions start with a 14-day free trial. No credit card information is required to register, and you can cancel anytime before the trial completes.

How does the team-size pricing calculate?

Our Professional tier scales dynamically at £12 per seat monthly. Our Enterprise plan scales at £24 per seat monthly. Starter tiers are free for up to 5 seats.

Is my company's data secure on Futura?

Security is our core priority. We use AES-256 database encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 encryption in transit, SOC 2 compliance certifications, and options to sync with your private cloud.
